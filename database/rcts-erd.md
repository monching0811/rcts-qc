## Key Relationships

```
rcts_citizen_registry (1)
    ├──< rcts_real_property          (one citizen → many properties)
    ├──< rcts_business_entity        (one citizen → many businesses)
    ├──< rcts_assessment_billing_hub (one citizen → many bills)
    ├──< rcts_payment_transaction    (one citizen → many payments)
    ├──< rcts_eor                    (one citizen → many receipts)
    ├──< rcts_aid_payout_registry    (one citizen → many payouts received)
    ├──< rcts_public_asset_stall     (one citizen → many stalls rented)
    └──< rcts_traffic_violation      (one citizen → many violations)

rcts_assessment_billing_hub (1)
    ├──< rcts_payment_transaction    (one bill → many payment attempts)
    └──< rcts_traffic_violation      (one bill ←→ one violation)

rcts_payment_transaction (1)
    ├── rcts_eor                     (one payment → one receipt)
    └──< rcts_treasury_ledger        (one payment → one ledger entry)

rcts_business_entity (1)
    └──< rcts_regulatory_clearance   (one business → many clearances)
```

## Auto-Calculated Columns (Zero Manual Math)
- `rcts_real_property.annual_tax_due` = assessed_value × 0.02
- `rcts_assessment_billing_hub.discount_amount` = base_amount × discount_rate
- `rcts_assessment_billing_hub.penalty_amount` = base_amount × penalty_rate
- `rcts_assessment_billing_hub.total_amount_due` = base - discount + penalty
- `rcts_treasury_dashboard.net_cash_position` = total_collected - total_disbursed
- `rcts_treasury_dashboard.target_variance` = total_collected - revenue_target
